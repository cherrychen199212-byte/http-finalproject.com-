Our Story

Lumière Noel was born from a passion for creating unforgettable holiday experiences. We believe that Christmas decorations should be more than just ornaments – they should be works of art that transform your home into a magical winter wonderland.

Our Mission

To provide the finest luxury Christmas decorations to Canadian homes, combining timeless elegance with exceptional quality and personalized service.

Curated Excellence: Every item in our collection is hand-selected for its quality, beauty, and craftsmanship.

Canadian Service Excellence: Based in Vancouver, we understand the unique needs of Canadian homes and celebrations. Our team provides personalized service, expert advice, and fast shipping across all provinces and territories.
